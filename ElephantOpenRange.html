Common name Scientific name Range and habitat Conservation status
African bush elephant Loxodonta africana Subsaharan Africa Vulnerable

Also known as the African savanna elephant, is the larger of the two species of African elephants, and the largest living terrestrial animal. These elephants were previously regarded as the same species, but the African forest elephant has been reclassified as L. cyclotis.
